    Help & Community

    • Community LearnDB and monster Lookup: Look up monster stats and short but up-to-date entries on game items & concepts in LearnDB. (alternate interface)
    • Community Wiki: Non-official community-run DCSS wiki, with a focus on describing the stable release
    • Forum: Debate strategy and propose changes on our forums
    • Chat: Our official IRC channel is #crawl on libera.chat, containing many players as well as the Sequell infobot. The dev channel (#crawl-dev) can also be found on this server. Many players can also be found chatting in #dcss on the Roguelikes Discord, and Cerebot there is bridged to Sequell.
    • Bug reports: Found a problem? Let us know! We also still accept reports on the mantis tracker, but user registration is closed.
    • Check out the source code, and fork or contribute!
    • Crawl Cosplay (CC): weekly CC Challenge, CC Academy and 2 tournaments
